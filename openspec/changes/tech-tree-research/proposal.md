## Why

Progression needs a tech tree to give players direction, create meaningful unlock gates, and reward exploration. The tree must feel scientifically grounded (hard sci-fi) while providing clear upgrade paths across mining, processing, robotics, and eventually the exotic research that unlocks late-game content — including the ability to make sense of anomalous signals.

## What Changes

- Define 5 research branches: **Mining Engineering** (tools, extraction efficiency), **Robotics** (new robot types, behavior upgrades), **Materials Science** (processing recipes, advanced materials), **Systems Integration** (automation, networking, AI tier unlocks), **Xenomineralogy** (unlocked by rare yield events; required for ancient-race content)
- Implement 60+ research nodes arranged in a DAG with explicit prerequisite chains
- Research is triggered by spending Research Points (RP) — generated by Survey robots and Science Labs
- Some nodes unlock via discovery (rare mineral drop, anomaly scan) rather than pure RP spend
- Research nodes grant: new robot types, new tools, new structures, new automation capabilities, efficiency bonuses, or narrative event triggers
- Add a research queue: player queues up to 5 pending research items; active research runs in the background
- Implement tech debt: some nodes have "rushed" mode (50% RP discount, 20% chance of partial failure causing a minor malfunction)

## Capabilities

### New Capabilities
- `research-node`: Individual research item with prerequisites, cost, unlock type, and reward payload
- `tech-tree-dag`: Directed acyclic graph of all research nodes with dependency resolution
- `research-queue`: Player-managed research queue with active item tracking and ETA display
- `rp-generation`: Research Point income from Survey robots, Science Labs, and anomaly discoveries
- `discovery-unlock`: Mechanism for research nodes triggered by in-game discoveries rather than RP spend

## Impact

- Gates all mid-to-late game content; balance is critical
- Xenomineralogy branch is a deliberate slow gate to ancient-race content — must feel earned, not arbitrary
- Discovery unlocks are the primary mechanical link between mining anomalies and narrative events
- All automation tier unlocks depend on Systems Integration branch
